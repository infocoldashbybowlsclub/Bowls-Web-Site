## Carlsberg v Cold Ashby
**Date:** Saturday, June 20th

### Cold Ashby Secure Solid Away Win in Historical Northampton Clash
Northampton — Cold Ashby Bowls Club braved the pre-match elements to secure a convincing 46-35 shot victory against Carlsberg, played at one of the town's most historic bowling venues. 

Despite an unsettling pre-match downpour that threatened to slick the green, the weather cleared just in time for the opening ends, remaining dry and clear throughout the contest. Cold Ashby found their rhythm early on the historic surface, establishing control across the rinks and maintaining a steady lead for the majority of the match. 

Carlsberg put up a spirited fight on their home turf, but Cold Ashby’s consistency proved too much, ultimately sealing an 11-shot victory. The standout performance of the day came from the trio of **Gerry, Ollie, and Michael**, who claimed the honor of highest winning rink with an excellent display of draw bowling that kept the pressure firmly on the hosts.

**Date:** Saturday 23rd & Sunday 24th May, 2026

## Club Competition Report: The Triples Cup
**Date:** Saturday, April 25th, 2026

**Overview**
The season kicked off in spectacular fashion as the club hosted its first official competition of the year: The Triples Cup. Players were treated to ideal conditions, competing under a clear blue sky that set the stage for a day of high-caliber play and spirited camaraderie.

**The Match**
The tournament lived up to the anticipation, proving to be an exceptionally tight contest. Momentum shifted several times throughout the afternoon, with neither side able to pull away significantly. The battle for the trophy came down to the final ends, characterized by precision shots and strategic positioning. In the end, the narrowest of margins decided the day, with only 3 shots separating the winners from the runners-up.

**Results**
Congratulations to our podium finishers who displayed fantastic teamwork and skill:

* **Winners:** Roger, Dean, & Pedro
* **Runners-up:** Ollie, Matt, & Harry

A huge thank you to everyone who participated and helped make the first competition of the season such a success. We look forward to seeing this level of competition continue throughout the year!
