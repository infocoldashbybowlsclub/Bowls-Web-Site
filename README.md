Cold Ashby Secure Solid Away Win in Historical Northampton Clash
Northampton — Cold Ashby Bowls Club braved the pre-match elements to secure a convincing 46-35 shot victory against Carlsberg, played at one of the town's most historic bowling venues.
Despite an unsettling pre-match downpour that threatened to slick the green, the weather cleared just in time for the opening ends, remaining dry and clear throughout the contest.
Cold Ashby found their rhythm early on the historic surface, establishing control across the rinks and maintaining a steady lead for the majority of the match. Carlsberg put up a spirited fight on their home turf, but Cold Ashby’s consistency proved too much, ultimately sealing an 11-shot victory.
The standout performance of the day came from the trio of Gerry, Ollie, and Michael, who claimed the honor of highest winning rink with an excellent display of draw bowling that kept the pressure firmly on the hosts.
